Common Topics

Disconnecting TikTok

Visit TikTok Manage App Permissions and revoke Reelr; we purge tokens within 30 days.

Request Data Deletion

Email privacy@reelr.ai from the address associated with your account and include your TikTok open_id (from profile). We will confirm deletion.

Status of Posts

The Service polls official TikTok status endpoints. If a video appears stuck >30 minutes, re‑initiate upload or contact support.
