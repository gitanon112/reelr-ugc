About Reelr

Mission

Empower creators & growth teams with transparent, compliant tooling to accelerate high‑quality short‑form video production and publication.

What We Provide

AI Assist

Optional generation (video / audio / music) with disclosure and review gates.

Posting Flexibility

Draft uploads for last‑mile edits, or direct posting once approved.

Compliance First

Least‑privilege scopes, fast revocation, rapid token purge on disconnect.

Principles

  • Transparency: AI generated media is labeled & optional.
  • Security: Encrypted storage, minimal retention, internal access logging.
  • Compliance: We follow TikTok guidelines; no scraping or unsupported automation.
