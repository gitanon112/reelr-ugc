Effective: 11 Aug 2025

1. Overview

Reelr ("we","us") provides a platform for authenticated TikTok creators to prepare, schedule, and post or draft short‑form videos.

2. Data We Collect

  • OAuth Profile: open_id, basic profile fields permitted by user.info.basic.
  • Posting Metadata: captions, scheduling times, selected privacy level.
  • Generated Media: Optional AI‑generated audio/video (stored in temporary cache before posting).
  • Operational Logs: Timestamps, API success/failure codes (no raw TikTok content beyond what is required).

3. How We Use Data

  • Authenticate and maintain session with TikTok APIs.
  • Prepare and submit posts/drafts at user request.
  • Provide scheduling and workflow UX.
  • Security auditing and abuse prevention.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We transmit media and metadata only to TikTok endpoints selected by the user. Infrastructure vendors (hosting, storage, monitoring) are bound by contractual processing terms.

5. Retention

Access & refresh tokens: stored encrypted; rotated per TikTok policy. Revoked tokens purged within 30 days. Media assets deleted automatically within 24h after successful post (or draft abandonment) unless user explicitly saves presets.

6. Security

Encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Principle of least privilege for internal services. Routine log review.

7. User Controls

  • Disconnect via TikTok’s Manage App Permissions.
  • Request deletion: email privacy@reelr.ai.
  • Revoke tokens inside Reelr UI (planned) or through support.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and other regions where our cloud infrastructure operates with appropriate safeguards.

9. Children

Not directed to children under 13 (or higher local age). We do not knowingly process such data.

Material changes posted here with updated effective date.
